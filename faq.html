FAQ

What makes GuideSignal different?

We highlight jobs that reply fast, show pay and must-haves up front, and send fewer, better intros timed for real responses.

Is it free for candidates?

Yes. Always free for candidates.

How do employers pay?

Posting is free. Employers can feature a job for $25 (one-time, 14 days) for top placement.

How do Fast-Reply (Beacon) badges work?

Badges are earned on measured data (median reply time & 24h reply rate) and can be revoked if performance slips.

Do you sell my data?

No. Forms go to Formspree; payments run on Stripe. We don't sell data, and we don't publish personal info.

Do I need a resume?

No. A 120–180 word Written Spotlight plus 2 links (projects, writing samples) works great.

How fast will I hear back?

We show live reply stats. Many Fast-Reply employers respond within hours; if not within 24h, we re-aim your outreach.

Can I remove my info?

Yes. Use the report/contact form; we delete outreach payloads within 30 days per our Privacy Policy.

Where are the jobs?

We start focused (one role × one city) and expand as response data grows to keep quality high.

How do I contact you?

Email us at ideamlabs@gmail.com for support, questions, or feedback.
