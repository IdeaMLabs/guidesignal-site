How GuideSignal Works

Lighthouse Model We shine a focused beam on jobs that reply fast and fit your skills/pay—so you spend time where a human will actually answer.

For Candidates (simple steps)

  1. Share your basics — city/remote, pay floor, skills, and a 120–180 word Written Spotlight with 2 links.
  2. See targeted roles — a short list ranked by fit, pay, and employer reply speed.
  3. One-tap outreach — we craft a brief email and send it when the employer usually answers.
  4. Clarity, not silence — if no response in 24h, we re-aim you to the next best option.
