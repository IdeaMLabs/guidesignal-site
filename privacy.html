Privacy Policy

How we protect and use your information

Data We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our matching service:

  • Candidates: Name, email, location, skills, experience, and pay expectations
  • Employers: Company name, contact details, job requirements, and response commitments
  • Usage Data: Application timestamps, response times, and matching analytics

How We Use Your Data

  • Matching: Connect qualified candidates with suitable employers
  • Communication: Facilitate introductions and follow-ups
  • Analytics: Track response times and platform effectiveness
  • Improvement: Enhance our matching algorithms and user experience
Security Promise: Employer contact information is encrypted at rest and transmitted securely. We never sell or share personal data with third parties.

Data Retention

  • Outreach Payloads: Deleted within 30 days of initial contact
  • Profile Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized data kept for service improvement
  • Communications: Email logs retained for 90 days for support purposes

Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request removal of your data from our systems
  • Portability: Export your data in a standard format

Data Sharing

We only share data:

  • Between matched candidates and employers (with consent)
  • With service providers under strict confidentiality agreements
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
